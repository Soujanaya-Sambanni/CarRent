Car Rental Policy

Introduction

Welcome to our car rental service! Before you proceed with renting a car, please take a moment to review our policy. This policy outlines important information about our terms and conditions, rental agreements, insurance coverage, and other essential details to ensure a smooth and enjoyable car rental experience.

Rental Terms and Conditions

  1. Minimum Age Requirement: The minimum age to rent a car is 21 years. Drivers below this age may be subject to additional fees or restrictions.
  2. Driver's License: All renters must possess a valid driver's license. International renters may be required to present an International Driving Permit (IDP) along with their license.
  3. Reservation and Payment: Reservations can be made online or through our customer service. Payment is required at the time of reservation and can be made using various accepted methods.
  4. Cancellation and Refunds: Cancellation policies may vary depending on the reservation type. Please refer to our website or contact our customer service for detailed information regarding cancellations and refunds.
  5. Rental Period: The rental period begins at the designated pickup time and ends at the agreed-upon return time. Late returns may incur additional charges.
  6. Fuel Policy: Renters are responsible for returning the vehicle with the same fuel level as when it was picked up. Additional charges may apply if the fuel level is not maintained.

Insurance Coverage

  1. Basic Insurance: Our rental rates include basic insurance coverage. This coverage typically includes third-party liability protection. Please refer to your rental agreement for specific details regarding insurance coverage.
  2. Additional Coverage: Additional insurance coverage, such as collision damage waiver (CDW) or loss damage waiver (LDW), can be purchased for added peace of mind. These options may reduce or eliminate your financial responsibility in case of damage or theft, subject to terms and conditions.

Vehicle Usage and Restrictions

  1. Prohibited Use: Renters are prohibited from using the rental vehicle for illegal activities, off-road driving, racing, or any other unauthorized purposes.
  2. Smoking and Pets: Smoking is strictly prohibited in our rental vehicles. Additionally, pets are generally not allowed unless prior arrangements have been made.

Damage and Liability

  1. Pre-rental Inspection: We conduct a pre-rental inspection to document the vehicle's condition. Renters are encouraged to inspect the vehicle thoroughly before taking possession and report any pre-existing damages to our staff.
  2. Damage Responsibility: Renters are responsible for any damage or loss incurred during the rental period. Please report any accidents or damages to our customer service immediately.
  3. Liability Limitations: Our liability is limited to the terms specified in the rental agreement. We recommend renters review the terms carefully and consider additional insurance coverage for optimal protection.

Payment and Charges

  1. Payment Methods: We accept various payment methods, including credit cards, debit cards, and cash. Please note that a valid credit card is required for the reservation, even if you plan to pay with a different method.
  2. Security Deposit: A security deposit will be held on your credit card at the time of rental to cover any potential damages or additional charges. The deposit amount will be released upon the return of the vehicle in satisfactory condition.
  3. Toll and Traffic Violations: Renters are responsible for any tolls, fines, or traffic violations incurred during the rental period. These charges will be billed to the renter's payment method.

Privacy and Data Protection

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. We collect and process your data in accordance with applicable data protection laws. For more information on how we handle your data, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

Customer Responsibilities

  1. Vehicle Care: Renters are expected to take care of the rental vehicle and return it in the same condition as when it was rented, excluding normal wear and tear.
  2. Additional Drivers: Additional drivers must be listed on the rental agreement and meet the same age and license requirements as the primary renter.
  3. Geographical Restrictions: Some rental agreements may have geographical restrictions. Please check the rental terms or consult with our staff to ensure compliance with any restrictions.

Dispute Resolution

In the event of any disputes or disagreements, we encourage renters to contact our customer service to attempt to resolve the issue. If a resolution cannot be reached, the matter may be escalated to appropriate legal channels as outlined in the rental agreement.

Please note that this policy is provided as a general guideline and may vary depending on the specific terms and conditions of your rental agreement. We recommend reviewing your rental agreement for complete details.

If you have any further questions or require clarification, please don't hesitate to contact our customer service. We are here to assist you and ensure a pleasant car rental experience.

Thank you for choosing our car rental service!
